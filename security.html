Safety & Security

Ensuring a protected environment where every child feels secure to learn, grow, and thrive

Our Safety Philosophy

Child-Centric Approach

Mount Litera management firmly believes that the optimum development of the child takes place in a safe and secure atmosphere and the desired goals can be achieved only if the mind is without fear.

Comprehensive Safety Policy

Safety for us is a comprehensive perspective. For this, school has a firm safety policy. Our safety policy ensures that each child feels that "I have the Right to Be myself and have the freedom to learn, work and play without having my heart, body and soul hurt".

100%
Safety Commitment

Round-the-clock protection

Security Features

Multi-layered security systems and protocols ensuring complete protection

24/7 Security

Round the clock security is provided by trained security personnel ensuring constant vigilance.

Identity Card System

Identity cards are issued to students as well as parents. Carrying Identity cards to school is mandatory for entry.

Biometric Attendance

Staff and students have biometric attendance facility for accurate tracking and security.

Bus Safety

Attendant and bus incharge teacher on every route. Speed governors installed in all the buses for controlled travel.

PA System

Central PA System installed for instant communication and emergency announcements throughout the campus.

Fire Safety

Fire Fighting System installed in each building with regular drills and maintenance checks.

CCTV Surveillance

Comprehensive CCTV system installed across campus for constant monitoring and security assurance.

Multi-Layered Protection

Physical security personnel at all entry/exit points

Digital access control systems

Regular safety audits and inspections

Emergency response protocols

First-aid trained staff members

Parent notification systems

Your Child's Safety is Our Priority

We maintain the highest standards of safety and security to ensure that every child learns in a protected, nurturing environment. Our comprehensive safety measures provide parents with peace of mind.
