Our Vision

Preparing Leaders for Tomorrow

Mount Litera Zee School will provide a complete and unique educational experience for the child, preparing him / her for a successful life in the contemporary society.

The vision of Mount Litera Zee School envisages:

  • To create an excellent educational institution synthesizing the human values with the highest quality of teaching-learning using modern technology-driven tools for preparing a well-rounded personality for our society.
