Clear Entry Guidelines

Entry Criteria for Admission

Admissions are granted to students on the basis of the following assessments:

Grade 1: age 5+ as on April 1

Grade 2 onwards: Followed by written test

Entrance test is based on previous class syllabus.

Internal transfers: Students of Mount Litera Zee School need not required to give any entrance test or assessment.

Documents Required

Documents to be submitted at the time of admission:

Birth Certificate

Issued by Govt. Authority

Progress Card

Previous academic year

Leaving Certificate

From previous school

Residence Proof

Valid address proof

Passport Size Photographs

Child, mother & father (2 each)
