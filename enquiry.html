Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common queries

When will I get a response?

Our team responds within 2-4 hours during working days. You'll receive a call and email confirmation.

What documents are needed?

Birth certificate, previous school reports, and address proof. We'll guide you through the process.

Can I visit the campus?

Yes! Schedule a campus tour through this form or call us directly to book your visit.

Is there an admission test?

For certain classes, yes. Our admission team will inform you about the specific requirements.
