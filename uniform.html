Important Instructions

Blazer Policy

Blazers are not compulsory for primary classes. Implementation depends on the school's discretion considering local climatic conditions.

Quality Standards

All uniform items must meet specified fabric ratios and color standards. Non-compliant uniforms will not be permitted.

Purchase Information

Uniforms available at school store (8 AM - 4 PM) and authorized vendors. Contact school for vendor details.

Regular Checks

Uniform checks will be conducted regularly. Students not adhering to uniform policy may face disciplinary action.
