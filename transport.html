Safe Transportation

School Transport

Safe, reliable, and efficient transportation services for our students

Important Notice for Parents

Parents are requested to kindly go through the following rules and guidelines regarding school bus transportation.

Bus Policy & Guidelines

Lady Escort on Every Bus

We provide a lady escort on the bus at all times for the safety and well-being of our students.

Punctuality Required

Children should be ready and waiting at their stops 5 minutes before pick up time.

Tight Schedule

Bus routes are very tightly scheduled so that children and teachers get to school on time. The buses WILL NOT wait for latecomers either at the stop or at school for the return trip.

Bus Communication

Each bus is equipped with a phone. Parents are encouraged to use this number when required:

Inform the escort on the bus if your child is not attending the school for some reason.
Check if the bus is running late (for the return trip). As there is a traffic jam for as long as 15 minutes often and it is not possible to inform every parent.

Route Changes

Any changes in pick up/drop off venue should be cleared with the office (in writing). The office will then inform the bus driver of the change.

Service Coverage

The buses cannot provide door-to-door service, as this increases the time spent commuting to and from school.

Bus Rules & Regulations

Engaging Activities

Different activities are conducted to make the best use of time during their journey in bus.

Safety First

Students should remain seated at all times and keep noise levels low.

Window Safety

Not stick hands or head out of the window for safety reasons.

Respectful Behavior

Not to be rude to people on the road and maintain proper conduct.

No Eating Policy

Not eat in the bus to maintain cleanliness and hygiene.

Cleanliness

Not litter things in the bus, failing which, penalties will be imposed, including exclusion from the bus service.
